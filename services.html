Our Expertise

Structured paths to peaceful resolution.

We provide a neutral, focused environment designed to de-escalate conflict and facilitate practical agreements. Explore our tailored mediation formats below.

Full-Day Mediation

Our primary service format, designed for comprehensive resolution of multi-faceted family law disputes. A full-day session allows the necessary time to untangle complex financial portfolios, address high-conflict custody arrangements, and navigate intense emotional dynamics without the pressure of a ticking clock.

  • 8 hours of dedicated, uninterrupted negotiation.
  • Ideal for high-asset property division and business valuations.
  • Deep-dive into sensitive child custody and visitation schedules.

Half-Day Mediation

A highly focused, four-hour session built for efficiency. Half-day mediations are best suited for cases where the parties have already reached consensus on major issues but require a formal setting to finalize details, or for modifications involving a limited scope of disputes.

  • 4 hours of concentrated negotiation (Morning or Afternoon).
  • Perfect for post-decree modifications or narrowed issues.
  • Cost-effective solution to avoid imminent trial dates.

Specialized Approach

Handling High-Conflict Dynamics

With over 40 years of trial experience, Steve Bruneman brings a unique capability to de-escalate severely fractured relationships and complex psychological factors that derail standard negotiations.

Mental Health & Addiction

Adept at structuring agreements that prioritize safety and stability when severe psychological issues, personality disorders, or substance abuse are central to the dispute.

High-Net-Worth Estates

Experienced in deciphering complex financials, business ownership structures, executive compensation packages, and tracing hidden assets for equitable division.

Severe Anger Dynamics

Utilizing separate rooms (caucusing) and structured communication to ensure neither party feels intimidated, maintaining focus on logic rather than reactive emotion.
