About Steve Bruneman

Decades of trial experience.
A profound focus on resolution.

Steve Bruneman is an AV Preeminent rated, board-certified family law attorney with an exceptional career spanning over four decades. As a trial lawyer since 1982 and a seven-time Texas SuperLawyer, Steve has handled every conceivable family law issue.

Having tried countless cases before courts and juries, he intimately understands the immense financial and emotional toll of litigation. This deep understanding of the psychological dynamics—especially concerning mental illness and severe anger issues—makes him exceptionally effective at managing parties' emotions during mediation.
