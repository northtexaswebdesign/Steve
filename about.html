The Mediator

A career built on the front lines of family law litigation.

Steve Bruneman began his legal career in 1982, quickly establishing himself as a formidable presence in Dallas family courts. For decades, he navigated the intense and often destructive waters of divorce litigation, representing clients in highly contentious custody battles and complex marital estate divisions.

However, witnessing the profound emotional and financial devastation that trials inflict on families led to a pivot in his philosophy. While remaining a fierce advocate when necessary, Steve recognized that the courtroom is rarely the best place to restructure a family's future.

Today, Steve dedicates his expertise exclusively to mediation. He leverages his extensive trial experience to offer parties a realistic assessment of their judicial alternatives. By understanding exactly how judges and juries react to specific facts, he helps parties move past entrenched positions toward equitable, self-determined settlements.

His deep understanding of the psychological dynamics inherent in divorce—particularly concerning personality disorders, addiction, and high-conflict personalities—makes him uniquely equipped to manage volatile emotions and keep negotiations constructive.

Our Approach

Why choose Bruneman Mediations?

01

Reality-Tested Solutions

We don't just facilitate conversation; we provide pragmatic reality checks. Drawing on decades of trial outcomes, we help parties understand the likely judicial alternatives to settlement, fostering more reasonable compromises.

02

Emotional De-escalation

Divorce is inherently emotional. Our structured mediation environments are designed to reduce volatility, separate the personal animosity from the legal issues, and keep the focus squarely on future stability.

03

Confidentiality & Control

Mediation keeps your family's private matters out of the public record. More importantly, it keeps the power of decision-making in your hands, rather than surrendering your future to a judge.
